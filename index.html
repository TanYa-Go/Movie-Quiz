How To Play
  • Choose your preferred difficulty and click 'PLAY'
  • You will be presented a question, with 4 possible answers; only one answer is correct!
  • When you select the answer, the correct answer will flash green.
  • On EASY difficulty you will have 25 seconds to answer, on NORMAL difficulty 20 seconds and on HARD you have only 15 seconds!
  • If the timer runs out, it's GAME OVER!
  • A correct answer will get you 10 points; incorrect answers get you 0 points.
  • A new question will automatically be loaded until you have answered all the questions
  • There are 10 questions per level
  • Restart Level button brings you back to the first question
  • Restart Game button allows you to start over and choose different level
