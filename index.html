How To Play
  • Choose the difficulty level first and then click Play
  • You will get a question with four possible answers
  • After you selected your answer, you will see if your answer was correct or not
  • On Easy level you have 20 seconds to answer, on Medium 15 and on Hard 10.
  • If the timer runs out, game is over!
  • For correct answer you will get 10 points and for incorrect you get no points
  • A new question will automatically be loaded until you have answered all the questions
  • There are 10 questions per level
