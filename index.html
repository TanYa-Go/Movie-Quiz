How To Play
  • Choose your preferred difficulty, and then click 'PLAY'
  • You will get a question with four possible answers
  • After you selected your answer, you will see if your answer was correct or not
  • On EASY difficulty you will have 25 seconds to answer, on NORMAL 20 seconds, and on HARD only 15 seconds!
  • If the timer runs out, it's GAME OVER!
  • A correct answer will get you 10 points; incorrect answers get you no points.
  • A new question will automatically be loaded until you have answered all the questions
  • There are 10 questions per level
